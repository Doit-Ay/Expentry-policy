Introduction

Welcome to Expentry! This Privacy Policy explains how Doitay ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information in relation to our mobile application "Expentry" (the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information and how we will treat it.

By downloading, accessing, or using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

Expentry is designed to help you track your expenses. The information we collect is primarily stored locally on your device.

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Receipt Details: When you add a receipt, you may provide information such as store name, purchase amount, date, category, notes, and an image of the receipt.
  • Images: If you use the image capture or gallery selection feature, we will require access to your device's camera or photo library to upload receipt images. These images are stored locally on your device as part of the receipt data managed by the app.

Information We Currently Do Not Collect:

(This list outlines data types generally not collected by the app as designed so far. If your app's functionality changes, this section must be updated.)

  • We currently **do not** require you to create a user account.
  • We currently **do not** collect personal identification information like your name (unless you voluntarily enter it in a receipt note), email address, or phone number for the core expense tracking functionality.
  • We currently **do not** collect precise geolocation data.
  • We currently **do not** use third-party analytics services that collect identifiable usage data tracking individual users.
  • We currently **do not** collect financial account numbers or payment card details. The "amount" field is for tracking expenses you enter manually.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To enable you to record, categorize, view, analyze, and manage your expense receipts directly on your device.
  • Local Storage: All expense data, including any images you attach to receipts, is stored locally on your device using your device's application storage mechanisms (e.g., AsyncStorage or internal storage). This data is not automatically transmitted to us or any third-party servers unless you explicitly initiate a backup or export feature (note: such features are not part of the current application design but may be added in the future, at which point this policy would be updated).
  • For App Functionality: To perform calculations for analytics (like total spending, category breakdowns) that are displayed to you within the app, on your device.

3. How We Share Your Information

Since all your expense data is stored locally on your device by default:

  • We **do not share your personal expense data or receipt images with any third parties** in a way that identifies you, unless you explicitly choose to use a feature that involves sharing (e.g., an export feature, or if you in the future opt-in to a cloud backup service, which would be covered by an updated policy and require your consent).

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your expense data (including text and images) is stored directly on your mobile device's local storage managed by the application.
  • Security: We rely on the security mechanisms of your mobile operating system to protect the data stored locally by the app. While we design the app with data protection in mind, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device (e.g., using passcodes, keeping your OS updated).
  • No Cloud Storage by Default: Expentry does not automatically back up or sync your data to any cloud servers owned or managed by Doitay. Any future cloud features would be opt-in and clearly explained.

5. Data Retention

Since data is stored locally on your device, it will remain there until:

  • You manually delete it from within the Expentry app.
  • You uninstall the Expentry app (which typically removes the app's local data, subject to your device's operating system behavior).

6. Your Rights and Choices

As your data is stored locally, you have direct control over it:

  • Access and Correction: You can access and modify your expense data directly within the Expentry app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual receipts or all data from within the app. Uninstalling the app will also typically remove its data from your device.
  • Permissions: You can manage app permissions (like Camera or Photo Library access) through your device's system settings at any time. Disabling permissions may limit or break certain app functionalities that depend on those permissions.
